Plot & Background

Avatar 2, directed by James Cameron, is the sequel to the highest-grossing film of all time. Released in December 2022, it continues the story of Jake Sully and Neytiri in the world of Pandora. The movie explores themes of environmentalism, family, and cultural unity.
